The Masters at Augusta National

The Masters Tournament, founded in 1934 by Bobby Jones and Clifford Roberts, is one of professional golf’s four major championships, held annually at Augusta National Golf Club. As the first major of the year, it is known for its exclusive, small field, the iconic Green Jacket awarded to winners, and signature traditions like the Champions Dinner.
